Slay the Spire - Game Knowledge Base

GAME OVERVIEW:
Slay the Spire is a deck-building roguelike game where players climb a spire, fighting enemies and collecting cards.

CHARACTERS:
- The Ironclad: Warrior character, focuses on strength and damage
- The Silent: Rogue character, uses poison and shivs
- The Defect: Robot character, uses orbs and powers
- The Watcher: Monk character, uses stances (Wrath, Calm)

COMBAT MECHANICS:
- Energy: Each turn you have 3 energy (can be increased)
- Cards: Play cards from your hand each turn
- Block: Reduces incoming damage
- Strength: Increases attack damage
- Dexterity: Increases block amount
- Poison: Deals damage at start of enemy turn
- Vulnerable: Enemy takes 50% more damage
- Weak: Enemy deals 25% less damage

COMMON ENEMIES:
- Cultist: Low health, buffs other enemies
- Jaw Worm: Moderate health, attacks and defends
- Louse: Low health, can apply debuffs
- Sentry: Moderate health, adds Dazed cards to your deck
- Exploder: Low health, explodes when killed dealing damage
- Large Gremlin: High health, strong attacks
- Spheric Guardian: High block, moderate health

KEY CARDS:
- Strike: Basic attack card, deals 6 damage
- Defend: Basic block card, blocks 5 damage
- Bash: Ironclad card, deals 8 damage and applies Vulnerable
- Neutralize: Silent card, deals 3 damage and applies Weak
- Zap: Defect card, channels 1 Lightning orb
- Eruption: Watcher card, deals 9 damage and enters Wrath stance

RELICTS:
- Relics provide passive bonuses
- Common examples: Burning Blood (heal 6 HP after combat), Ring of the Snake (draw 2 extra cards turn 1)

UI ELEMENTS:
- Health bar: Shows current HP / max HP
- Energy: Number in bottom right shows available energy
- Hand: Cards you can play this turn
- Draw pile: Cards not yet drawn
- Discard pile: Cards already played
- Exhaust pile: Cards removed from combat

COMBAT STATUS:
- Look for enemy intents (what they plan to do next turn)
- Block before attacks to reduce damage
- Manage energy efficiently
- Plan multi-turn strategies
