Tic Tac Toe - Game Knowledge Base

GAME OVERVIEW:
Tic Tac Toe is a simple two-player game played on a 3x3 grid.

RULES:
- Players take turns placing X or O marks
- First player to get 3 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins
- If all 9 squares are filled with no winner, it's a tie

BOARD LAYOUT:
- 3 rows, 3 columns = 9 total squares
- Positions can be described as: top-left, top-center, top-right, middle-left, middle-center, middle-right, bottom-left, bottom-center, bottom-right
- Or using coordinates: (0,0) to (2,2) or (1,1) to (3,3)

WINNING CONDITIONS:
- Three X's or O's in a row horizontally
- Three X's or O's in a row vertically  
- Three X's or O's diagonally (top-left to bottom-right or top-right to bottom-left)

STRATEGY:
- Center square is most valuable
- Corners are second most valuable
- Block opponent's winning moves
- Create two threats simultaneously when possible

UI ELEMENTS:
- Grid: 3x3 board with squares
- X marks: Player 1's moves
- O marks: Player 2's moves
- Empty squares: Available moves
- May show whose turn it is
- May show game status (in progress, X wins, O wins, tie)

GAME STATES:
- In progress: Game still ongoing
- X wins: Three X's in a row
- O wins: Three O's in a row
- Tie: Board full, no winner
