Stardew Valley - Game Knowledge Base

GAME OVERVIEW:
Stardew Valley is a farming simulation game where players manage a farm, interact with villagers, and explore the world.

LOCATIONS:
- Farm: Your home base, where you grow crops and raise animals
- Pelican Town: Main town with shops and villagers
- Town Square: Central area with shops and events
- Stardew Valley Fair: Annual festival with games and competitions
- Mines: Underground areas with monsters and resources

FARMING:
- Crops: Plant seeds, water daily, harvest when ready
- Seasons: Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter (different crops each season)
- Tools: Hoe (till soil), Watering Can (water crops), Axe (chop trees), Pickaxe (mine)
- Animals: Cows, chickens, pigs provide resources (milk, eggs, truffles)

VILLAGERS:
- NPCs you can befriend and give gifts
- Each has a schedule and favorite items
- Building relationships unlocks cutscenes and benefits

FESTIVALS:
- Stardew Valley Fair: Annual fall festival
- Ice Fishing Contest: Winter event
- Various seasonal festivals throughout the year

UI ELEMENTS:
- Inventory: Bottom of screen shows items you're carrying
- Health/Energy: Top left shows your health and energy bars
- Time: Top right shows current time and day
- Money: Top right shows your gold
- Toolbar: Bottom shows equipped tools and items

GAMEPLAY MECHANICS:
- Energy: Depletes when using tools, restore with food or sleep
- Health: Can be lost in mines, restore with food
- Time: Day runs from 6 AM to 2 AM, must sleep by 2 AM
- Skills: Farming, Mining, Foraging, Fishing, Combat

COMBAT (in Mines):
- Monsters appear in mines
- Use sword to attack
- Health bar shows remaining HP
- Can die and lose items/money if health reaches zero
